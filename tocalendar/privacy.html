Privacy Policy for ToCalendar

Last updated: September 13, 2025

Information We Collect

ToCalendar is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users.

Local Data Processing

All document scanning and calendar integration happens locally on your device. Your documents, calendar data, and extracted events never leave your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

ToCalendar may use AI services for document analysis. When using these services, only the content of documents you choose to scan is processed. No personal information or calendar data is included in these requests.

Subscription Data

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Apple handles all subscription management and billing according to their privacy policy.

Calendar Access

ToCalendar requests access to your calendar solely to add events you approve. We do not read, modify, or access existing calendar events beyond what is necessary for duplicate detection.

Data Storage

Any temporary data created during document processing is stored locally on your device and automatically deleted after processing is complete.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at [your email].

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or this website.
