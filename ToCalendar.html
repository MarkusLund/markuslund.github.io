Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use ToCalendar?

ToCalendar is an intelligent calendar app that scans your files and automatically generates calendar events that you can seamlessly add to your Apple Calendar. The app helps you organize your appointments and tasks by extracting relevant information from your documents.

How do I contact support?

You can contact us at support@haavelund.no for all questions related to the app. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
